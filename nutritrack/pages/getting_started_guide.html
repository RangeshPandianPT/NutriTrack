Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from new users to help you get started faster

How accurate is the food database?

Our database contains over 11 million foods with nutritional data verified by registered dietitians. We continuously update and improve accuracy based on user feedback.

Do I need to count every macro?

Not necessarily! Start with calories and protein, then gradually add carbs and fats as you become more comfortable with tracking.

What if I eat out frequently?

Our database includes menu items from 500+ restaurant chains. For local restaurants, use our estimation tools or similar food matches.

How do I interpret my progress data?

Focus on weekly trends rather than daily fluctuations. Our analytics highlight patterns and provide actionable insights for improvement.

Can I sync with my fitness tracker?

Yes! We integrate with Fitbit, Apple Health, Garmin, and other popular fitness devices to automatically sync your activity data.

What if I miss logging a day?

Don't worry! Consistency matters more than perfection. Just resume logging the next day and focus on building the habit gradually.
