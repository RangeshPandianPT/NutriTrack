Best protein sources for vegetarians?

I'm new to vegetarianism and struggling to meet my protein goals. What are the best plant-based protein sources that taste good and are easy to prepare?

Dr. Sarah Johnson Expert

Great question! Focus on legumes (lentils, chickpeas), quinoa, tofu, tempeh, and nuts. Aim for 20-25g protein per meal. Try my favorite: lentil curry with quinoa - delicious and complete protein!
